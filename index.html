What is LMS?

Leiomyosarcoma (LMS) is a rare type of cancer that affects smooth muscle tissue. It is estimated that LMS accounts for less than 1% of all cancer diagnoses, making it one of the rarer forms of sarcoma. While the cause is often unknown, LMS can develop in soft tissues such as muscles, fat, blood vessels, and organs. Early detection and treatment are key to improving quality of life for those affected.
