What is LMS?

Leiomyosarcoma (LMS) is a rare cancer that affects smooth muscle tissue, found in organs such as the uterus, stomach, and blood vessels. LMS accounts for less than 1% of all cancer diagnoses, making it a rare but serious condition. Early diagnosis and research advancements are helping patients manage and treat LMS more effectively.
