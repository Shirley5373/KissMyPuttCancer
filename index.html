About Jennifer Jane Pope

Jennifer Jane Pope is a loving, courageous woman currently fighting against leiomyosarcoma. Her strength and resilience inspire everyone around her. This event supports her journey and others facing similar battles.

What is Leiomyosarcoma?

Leiomyosarcoma (LMS) is a rare and aggressive form of cancer arising from smooth muscle tissue. It is resistant to chemotherapy and requires specialized treatment. LMS highlights the need for awareness, research, and support for those affected.
